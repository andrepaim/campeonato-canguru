## src/data/schedule.ts
Opponent rotation: 19 games per round, weaker teams first.
OPPONENT_ORDER = ['cuiaba','criciuma','vitoria','atletico-go','juventude','bragantino','fortaleza','bahia','vasco','cruzeiro','athletico-pr','botafogo','internacional','gremio','sao-paulo','corinthians','fluminense','palmeiras','flamengo']
getOpponentForMatchDay(matchDay): index into OPPONENT_ORDER with ((matchDay-1) % 19)

## src/utils/questions.ts
selectMatchQuestions(allQuestions, matchDay, usedIds): 2 easy + 2 medium + 2 hard
Seeded shuffle: use matchDay as seed, deterministic

## src/utils/scoring.ts
- calculateGoals(timeMs, isCorrect): 2 if correct+timeMs<10000, 1 if correct+slow, 0 if wrong/skip
- simulateOpponentGoals(seed): 0,1,1,1,2,2,2,3 weighted random based on seed
- calculateResult(home, away): {result: 'W'|'D'|'L', points: 3|1|0}
- initStandings(teams): all zeros
- updateStandings(standings, match): updates CAM entry
- simulateRound(standings, matchDay): simulates all other 19 team results with pseudo-random outcomes

## src/store/championshipStore.ts
Zustand store with localStorage persist ('campeonato-canguru-v1'):
State: matchDay(starts 1), lastPlayedDate(null), matches[], standings(initialized), usedQuestionIds[], currentMatch(null), currentQuestions([]), currentAnswers([])
Actions: canPlayToday(), startMatch(opponentId, questions), submitAnswer(qId, answer, timeMs), skipQuestion(qId), finalizeMatch(), resetAll()

## src/pages/HomePage.tsx
- Header: вҡҪ + "Campeonato Canguru" + current round "Rodada X"

## src/pages/MatchPage.tsx
- Fetch questions on load (from store)
- Scoreboard: CAM_emoji SCORE:SCORE OPP_emoji, "QuestГЈo N/6"
